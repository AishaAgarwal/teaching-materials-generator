About TeachPrep AI

Our Mission

TeachPrep AI is an AI-powered educational content generator designed to help teachers, trainers, and educators create comprehensive teaching materials in minutes instead of hours.

We've combined the power of modern AI with an intuitive interface to create a tool that truly saves educators time and enhances the quality of educational materials.

How It Works

1

Input Your Topic

Enter your subject area, grade level, and specific topic that you want to teach. Add any specific requirements or focus areas.

2

Customize Content

Our AI generates a comprehensive set of teaching materials that you can review and customize. Edit topics, add your own content, and refine the generated materials.

3

Download & Present

Export your customized content as a PowerPoint presentation, ready to use in your classroom or training session. You can also save your work for future reference.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes, we do not store any of your generated content on our servers. All generated content is saved locally in your browser's storage and can be cleared at any time.

Can I customize the generated content?

Yes, our editor allows you to fully customize all aspects of the generated content. You can edit topics, add your own content, and refine the generated materials.

What topics can I generate materials for?

You can generate teaching materials for virtually any subject or topic. The AI is trained on a wide range of educational content and can create materials for technical subjects, humanities, sciences, and more.

Is this tool free to use?

This is a demonstration application using the OpenAI API. For production use, you would need your own OpenAI API key.
